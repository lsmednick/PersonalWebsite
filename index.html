Biography

Logan S. Mednick is an audio and software engineer currently based in Boston, MA. He graduated cum laude from Ithaca College with a B.M. in Sound Recording Technology, and is currently pursuing an M.S. in Computer Science at Northeastern University. Logan's work experience in audio has covered a wide range of mediums such as studio recording, live sound engineering, and post production. Most recently, Logan has served as a freelance audio engineer for Distribimage, a company specializing in english overdubs for various animated television programs. After working professionally in audio, Logan decided to begin studying computer science in order to successfully transition to a career in software engineering. Logan is currently a software engineer at Bankers Healthcare Group and plans to finish his masters degree in 2022.
